About Me

My Story

My name is Erin Yeonjae Choi. I am an industrial designer who listens to what users need and want. I think it is most important to not assume what clients will like or feel when designing but work based on the actual research data. It is something that has to be heard before prototyping. I am proficient in SOLIDWORKS and Rhino. As an industrial designer, I pay extra attention to details and believe that those small details make huge differences and contribute to the final look.
