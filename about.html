About Me

My Story

My name is Erin Yeonjae Choi and I am a junior Industrial Designer. I came to Vancouver, Canada to study abroad in 2013, and I am currently an undergraduate student at SFU, majoring in Interactive Arts and Technology. As I started exploring design, I found Industrial Design fun and enjoyable. I got fascinated to see how a 3-Dimensional digital model can be created into an actual physical model.

When I design, I pay attention to details because I believe that small details make huge differences and complete a final design. I also try to achieve both aesthetic forms and functionality. I am proficient in SOLIDWORKS and Rhino. I have experience in model making using various materials, such as cardboard, glossy cardstock, styrene, and foam. I look forward to working on new projects and getting more experience in Industrial Design.
